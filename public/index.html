How many of you do you know someone who has falling into debt that they cannot get out of. Maybe a family member who can't pay their medical bills, a friend who can't find a job, or a random person you can get back on their feet. ¢hange for a ¢ause will help you by letting you round up your online purchases to the next dollar amount and then depositing that change into the account of your choosing.

Why is this different then donating for a cause at the grocery store? For one we won't make you feel bad by asking you 'Do you want help cure cancer?' or similar questions that make you feel like your horrible for not helping. Instead it's all up to you and its only for causes or people you truly want to help. Simply make an account with us, choose the patron you want to fund, and bump up your online purchases whenever you can afford it.

FAQ

What websites support ¢hange for a ¢ause?
Currently none.
How do I set myself up to receive donations?
Create an online profile with us and share your profile via Facebook or other social networks.
How do you make sure people are being scammed?
First you get to choose who you donate to. If you don't trust them then don't pick them. If you are donating to random people we can assure you we verify they are in financial need and only verified patrons can receive random donations.
What happens when my patron no longer needs monies?
Once a patron has received their financial goal, further donations will be refunded to your account and they will no longer have an active donation account.
